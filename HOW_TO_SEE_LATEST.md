### Mobile Browsers

#### iPhone / Safari
**Option 1 - Private Mode:**
1. Open Safari
2. Tap the tabs button (bottom-right)
3. Tap "Private" (bottom-left)
4. Open the link in private mode

**Option 2 - Clear Cache:**
1. Go to **Settings** ‚Üí **Safari**
2. Tap **"Clear History and Website Data"**
3. Confirm
4. Re-open the link

#### Android / Chrome
**Option 1 - Incognito:**
1. Open Chrome
2. Tap menu (3 dots)
3. Tap "New incognito tab"
4. Open the link in incognito

**Option 2 - Clear Cache:**
1. Tap menu (3 dots) ‚Üí **Settings**
2. Tap **Privacy** ‚Üí **Clear browsing data**
3. Select "Cached images and files"
4. Tap "Clear data"
5. Re-open the link
